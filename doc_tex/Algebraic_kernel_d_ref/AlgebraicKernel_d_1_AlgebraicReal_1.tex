\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::AlgebraicReal_1}

\ccDefinition

A model of \ccRefName\ is used to represent real roots of a univariate 
polynomial \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Polynomial_1}. 

A model of \ccRefName\ is required to be \ccc{ImplicitInteroperable} 
with \ccc{int}, \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Coefficient}, 
and \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Bound}, that is, it is possible to 
construct from these types and to compare against these types. 

\ccRefines
\ccc{DefaultConstructible}\\
\ccc{CopyConstructible}\\
\ccc{Assignable}\\
\ccc{FromIntConstructible}\\
\ccc{RealEmbeddable}\\
\ccc{ImplicitInteroperable} with \ccc{int}\\
\ccc{ImplicitInteroperable} with \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Coefficient}\\
\ccc{ImplicitInteroperable} with \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Bound}\\


In addition to the default and copy constructors, as well as the
constructor from int, an \ccRefName\ is also constructible from a
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Coefficient}:

\ccCreationVariable{ar1}
\ccConstructor{AlgebraicReal_1(AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Coefficient c)}{}
