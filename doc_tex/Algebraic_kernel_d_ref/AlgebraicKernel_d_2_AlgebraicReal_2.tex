\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_d_2::AlgebraicReal_2}

\ccDefinition

A model of \ccRefName\ is used to represent real roots of two polynomial 
equations in two variables \ccc{x} and \ccc{y} that are models of 
concept \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d::Polynomial_2}. 

A model of \ccRefName\ is required to be \ccc{LessThanComparable} with respect to 
the lexicographic order according to \ccc{x} and \ccc{y}.

\ccRefines
\ccc{DefaultConstrctible}\\
\ccc{CopyConstructible}\\
\ccc{Assignable}\\
\ccc{EqualityComparable}\\
\ccc{LessThanComparable}
