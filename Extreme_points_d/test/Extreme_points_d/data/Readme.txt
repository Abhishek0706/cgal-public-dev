Format of the Synthetic Data Files: “YYbyXXXXXatZZ.con”, “YY” indicates the dimension of the point set (e.g., 05); “XXXXX” is the cardinality (number of points, e.g., 05000); and “ZZ” is the extreme point density (e.g., 25 means 25% of the points are extreme).
