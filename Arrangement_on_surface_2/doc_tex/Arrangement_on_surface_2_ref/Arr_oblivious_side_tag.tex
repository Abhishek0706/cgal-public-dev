\begin{ccRefClass}{Arr_oblivious_side_tag}

\ccDefinition
%============

The categories \ccc{Left_side_category}, \ccc{Right_side_category},
\ccc{Bottom_side_category}, and \ccc{Top_side_category},
nested in any model of the \ccc{ArrangementBasicTraits_2}, must be
convertible to \ccRefName. \ccRefName{} is an empty construct used
for dispatching functions based on type of curves that induce the
arrangement.
