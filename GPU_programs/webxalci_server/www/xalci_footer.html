Polynomials can be entered in both rational and floating-point formats, coefficients of arbitrary length are accepted:
for instance, 1e-3(x+2y)-5/1234(1.45y^2+x^2y)-0.0001 is a valid input.

To see more examples of algebraic curves and arrangements of such, visit our Curve gallery. Web-application design and server software by Pavel Emeliyanenko.
